01

Olist Seller Performance Monitor

Built an end-to-end operational monitoring tool for Olist's Seller Success team — covering 96K delivered orders, 3K sellers, and 71 product categories across Brazil. The pipeline segments sellers into performance tiers and surfaces who needs intervention, why they're underperforming, and what separates top performers from the rest.

Python pandas seaborn Tableau 96K orders 3K sellers
GitHub Repo → Live Dashboard →
Key findings
  • Dispatch speed is the dominant driver of review scores — top sellers hand off packages twice as fast as low-performers
  • High GMV does not predict quality: the 2nd-largest seller by revenue sits in the Low tier with a 25% negative review rate
  • Freight cost is essentially uncorrelated with satisfaction (Spearman r = −0.047) — delivery reliability matters far more
  • Category problems split into two types: operational failures vs. structural mismatches — each requiring a different intervention

02

RavenStack — SaaS Churn & Retention Analysis

Built a churn-risk monitoring tool for a B2B SaaS pilot — 500 accounts over a 24-month window. Combines cohort retention, pre-churn behavioral signals, and segment-level LTV, then scores every active account with a logistic-regression risk model so a VP of Customer Success can see who is at risk, why, and where revenue is most exposed.

Python pandas scikit-learn seaborn Tableau 500 accounts 24-mo pilot
GitHub Repo → Live Dashboard → Write-up →
Key findings
  • Early tenure is the dominant churn signal — the first-month cohort drop (15.7pp) and short tenure outweigh every other predictor in the risk model
  • Churners show higher product usage in their final 60 days than retained accounts — disengagement isn't the warning sign; usage density relative to tenure is
  • Gross revenue retention holds at 97.8% even as roughly half of logos churn by month 12 — revenue concentrates in a stable, multi-subscription Enterprise base
  • Ads-acquired accounts retain best (57.7% churn) while organic churns worst (73.4%) despite the highest LTV — channel quality doesn't track acquisition volume
